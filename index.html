About

Hi everyone! I’m Vishakha, an Interior Designer passionate about creating spaces that are not only beautiful but also functional and people-centric. With hands-on experience in both residential and commercial projects,

I specialize in blending comfort, aesthetics, and innovation. My skills include space planning, concept development, material selection, and design presentation, allowing me to deliver cohesive and impactful interiors. My design philosophy revolves around enhancing functionality while maintaining visual harmony.

My work is guided by a deep understanding of color theory, materials, textures, and the core principles of design. From learning design fundamentals to applying them during real-world internships, I’ve grown into someone who can transform concepts into meaningful, functional space .

skills

AutoCAD

I have strong experience in drafting detailed floor plans and technical drawings using AutoCAD. I can translate design concepts into accurate, construction-ready layouts with precision. My workflow focuses on clarity, accuracy, and well-organized documentation.

Google SketchUp

I use SketchUp to build realistic 3D models that help visualize space, scale, and design concepts effectively. I’m skilled at developing clean, well-structured models that communicate ideas clearly. My models support both client presentations and detailed design development.

Adobe Photoshop

I work with Photoshop to enhance renders, refine textures, and create visually appealing presentation boards. I’m comfortable editing images, correcting colors, and designing clean layouts. My edits help communicate design ideas more clearly and professionally.

Microsoft Office

I use Microsoft Office to create well-structured project documents, schedules, and client presentations. I’m efficient in organizing information in a clean, readable format. My documentation supports smooth communication throughout the project.

Enscape

I create high-quality real-time renders and walkthroughs using Enscape. I’m skilled at producing lighting-accurate scenes that bring design concepts to life. My Enscape visuals help clients experience the space before it’s built.

ZWCAD

I use ZWCAD to draft precise 2D drawings and technical layouts for interior projects. I focus on clarity, accuracy, and properly labeled documentation. My drawings help ensure smooth execution and clear communication on-site.
